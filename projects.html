Audio Visualization Machine
Enter here

Tools: HTML, CSS, p5.js, GitHub, audio recorder, GarageBand
A machine that translates background sounds of my favorite time of the day, dawn. When all is quiet in my little apartment except for the crinkling of a bread bag or the bubbling of water boiling in the kettle. I recorded these familiar sounds and coded a program that visualizes the frequency waves in each mp3 file. Enjoy!

Soviet Archive
Enter here

Tools: HTML, CSS, GitHub Pages
This project is a digital archive of family portraits that I created to memorialize my family's history in the Soviet Union through their immigration to the United States. I'm deeply inspired by the stories of my ancestors and wanted to build a website that housed these keepsakes for as long as the World Wide Web exits.
